Cake Recipe

Description

It is just a recipe of a chocolate cake

Ingridients

  • All-Purpose Flour: 4 tablespoons
  • Sugar: 2 tablespoons
  • Unsweetened Cocoa Powder: 2 tablespoons
  • Baking Powder: 1/4 teaspoon
  • Milk: 3 tablespoons
  • Vegetable Oil: 1 tablespoon
  • Vanilla Extract: 2 drops
  • Chocolate Chips: 1 tablespoon (optional, but highly recommended for a gooey center)

Steps

  1. Mix Dry: Grab your favorite standard-sized ceramic mug. Add the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, and baking powder. Stir with a fork until there are no clumps.
  2. Mix Wet: Add the milk, oil, and vanilla. Whisk with the fork until the batter is smooth.
  3. The Secret Heart: Drop the chocolate chips right into the center of the batter. Don't stir them in; let them sink naturally to create a molten core.
  4. Microwave: Cook on high for 90 seconds.(Note: Every microwave is different. If the top looks wet, give it another 10â€“15 seconds.)
  5. Cool: Let it sit for at least 2 minutes. This is the hardest part, but it finishes the cooking process and prevents you from burning your tongue!
