Bread Recipe

Description

Hey this is just a bread recipe

Ingridients

  • All-Purpose or Bread Flour: 1 cup
  • Warm Water: 1/3 cup + 1 tablespoon
  • Instant Yeast: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Salt: 1/4 teaspoon
  • Honey or Sugar: 1/4 teaspoon (optional, helps browning)

Step

  1. Mix: In a medium bowl, stir the flour, salt, and yeast together. Pour in the warm water (and honey if using). Stir with a spoon until a sticky dough ball forms and no dry flour remains.
  2. First Rise: Cover the bowl with a plate or plastic wrap. Let it sit in a warm spot for 1.5 to 2 hours. It should double in size and look bubbly.
  3. Shape: Scrape the dough onto a floured surface. Fold the edges into the center a few times to form a ball. Place it on a small piece of parchment paper.
  4. Second Rise: Let the ball rest for 30 minutes while you preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  5. Bake: Slide the parchment paper with the dough onto a baking sheet. Just before closing the oven door, pour half a cup of water into that empty tray on the bottom rack (this creates steam for a crispy crust!).
  6. Timing: Bake for 20–25 minutes until the top is golden brown and sounds hollow when tapped on the bottom.
